Submission

For NITS 2023, we welcome contributions on all aspects related to translation studies in the broadest sense. Submissions can take the form of: - 20-minute paper presentations on educational or research projects (current and future) - posters of educational or research projects (current and future) - 10-minute practice reports on best practices and lessons learnt We welcome contributions from early-career researchers as well as MA and PhD students! Abstracts should be max. 300 words (excl. references), clearly indicate the type of submission (paper, poster or practice report) and can be written in English or Dutch. Please submit your abstract through Easychair, using the following link:

https://easychair.org/account/signin?l=M5JNDWtsO8WfI3FJwImjUc

You can also already register for the meeting, free of charge!
